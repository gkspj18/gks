ABOUT GKS ASSOCIATES

Creating Exceptional Spaces for Over Three Decades

GKS Associates is a Chennai-based architecture and project management consultancy led by Ganeshmurthy G. With more than 30 years of experience, we specialize in hospitality, luxury hotels, resorts, commercial developments, residential projects and sustainable design.

Our expertise spans architecture, interiors, project management consultancy, construction supervision, MEP coordination and renovation works, ensuring every project is delivered with quality, efficiency and attention to detail.
